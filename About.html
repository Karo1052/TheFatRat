Upbringing

Christian Friedrich Johannes Büttner (also known as TheFatRat) was born June 1th 1979 in Göttingen, Germany.

Music carrier

His music carrier started in 2001 with him creating background music for tv, commercials etc. In 2011 he started making music by the name TheFatRat. His carrier bursted on Youtube and his music has also been used in more than 1.5 million other Youtube videos. His second EP Jackpot reached number 23 on the Billboard Dance/Electric Album chart. He started a record company called The Arcadium in collab with Universal Music in December 2016. He is now signed onto the recordcompany Casablanca Records. In 2017 he released the album Warrior Songs witch is an album inspired by the game Dota 2.
